    • I am

    My name is Eugene Zhu, and I am an upper year student studying Interactive Arts and Technology (SIAT) at Simon Fraser University.

    I am skilled in various design fields but have a focus on UX/UI Design. I am passionate about creating unique products and features for various sized companies that put the user first.

    Ever since I was a little kid, I have always been interested in the creative arts field. Being able to express myself through my own creations and knowing that I created something unique is what really excited me and inspired me to continue pursuing this field. As well, being able to create things that other people really enjoyed made me happy.

    As I grew older and started looking into careers that I wanted to pursue, I stumbled into the UX Design field during my later high school years, and was instantly hooked. Being able to pursue my childhood passion of the creative arts was one thing, but also being able to utilize my creativity to design experiences that will help other people was what really caught my attention and made me want to follow this career path.

    In the art world, there's a saying that a piece of artwork is never perfect. As someone who has been dabbling in visual art since childhood, I resonate very closely with this saying, and I approach products much the same way. I am always looking for new ways to improve things through feedback and research, and being able to connect the dots from the behaviours of users to creating an experience that benefits them is something that I really enjoy, and helps me connect with them on a personal level through design.

    Outside of design, I also love learning and improving my skills in other creative fields, such as art, programming, and photography.

    If you would like to contact me or learn more about my portfolio and design process, feel free to reach out by email at Eugene.Zhu2000@gmail.com or LinkedIn at https://www.linkedin.com/in/eugenezhu/.

    My Values

    📖 Never stop learning!

    The design field is constantly changing, which is quite evident seeing how design trends have changed over the years. As a designer with a large skillset, I embrace this change and strive to learn new ways for me to contribute to the design field. I believe in the continual learning process and am always willing to try new things.

    🤯 Creativity is key!

    I love thinking outside the box, and I believe that there is no better industry than design to do so. I love thinking of new and unique ways to improve the features and user experience for products by combining my broad technical skills with my creative skills to do so.

    💖 Be passionate!

    As designers, we communicate to our users through our designs, so our passion translates into what we create. Without passion, we become less creative and less innovative. When I design, I always keep a positive mindset and focus on creating something of value to others.
