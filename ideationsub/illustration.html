    Description

    Illustrate a high-detailed artwork for the commissioner.

    Roles/Tools

    • Research - Google
    • Sketches/Illustration - Photoshop

    About

    Throughout the years, I have made countless illustrations in both traditional and digital mediums. This artwork was made for one of my friends as a request for an event that took place at the time I made this illustration.

    Brainstorming/Research Phase

    To start the project off, I asked my friend to find visuals online that they wanted the artwork to convey. After getting the visuals, I made a mood board with them that I could follow while drawing. From the images I've gotten and their requests, I could tell that also requested their character be drawn with a vibrant background that was ocean-themed, with clouds and the background as the main focus.

    First Iteration: Rough Sketch

    I then move on to doing a rough sketch of the illustration. During this section of the design process, I stay in touch with the commissioner, sending them process pictures of my sketch so that everything from the background to the character pose is accurate with how the commissioner envisioned the final piece. I typically make a few iterations with the commissioner before the final sketch is achieved.

    Second Iteration: Final Illustration

    Once the sketch is done, I move on to the actual content of the illustration, referencing my mood board throughout the process to keep the concept of the piece in mind. I begin the process by finalizing the sketch of the characters so that I have a general Idea of which sections are visible and need to be illustrated in the background.
