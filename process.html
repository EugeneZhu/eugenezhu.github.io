Case Studies

In this section, I will review the design process for my projects.

If I had to use one word to describe my design process, it would be collaborative. As a user-centric designer, I use the design process as a method to make a work that connects me with the people who will use my product. I want the users to know that I am making a product for them, not for myself.

Below are documentations of my design process for various projects. If you would like to see more of my completed projects, please go to the projects section of website.
